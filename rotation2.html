Print/Dye


My first task was shirt design and print.  I used photoshop to create this
pirate/cowboy design.  I exported this as a png and imported it into the 
t-shirt printing app, where I centered it and then exported it. I brought
the file to the Brother printer on a flashdrive and printed it.
			



My second task was using dye-sublimation and I decided to make an apron.
I once again used photoshop and then simply took it to the dye/sub printer
and printed out the printed design that would eventually bleed through my
apron using the heat of the heat press. To prep, I centered it and taped it 
down to the apron with heat resistant tape.  The process worked great besides
a dye-run off line from the previous press that ended up on my apron.  I made a
black line to cover it up.
