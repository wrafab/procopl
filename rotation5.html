Vinyl/Milling


For this rotation, we had to first create stickers and decals.
These were two completely different processes.  The sticker was
very simple and was simply a design in coreldraw, an outline, 
an export, and a print button.  It peels off the backing like
a normal sticker.  The decal, on the other hand, was very complicated,
having all kinds of tools and methods behind getting a simple image on
paper. Heres my sticker and decal:
		

The second part of this rotation was the milling section of it.  Part of
it was a laser-cut, coin mold, which I simply did in coreldraw and then
laser cut myself.  The mold which we later poured chocolate in was a design
from fusion 360 that I send to Mr. Gerber, who later milled that out for us.
