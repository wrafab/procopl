3D Printed Wax Stamp





Process

Fusion360

In Fusion360, I created the stl for this wax stamp.  
Starting with 2d geometric shapes, I extruded them to their 
current form. I then traced the Fleur de Lis and "P" with 
line segments in order to extrude those.  Finally, I added filets
for a design element.
