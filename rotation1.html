• 3D Printed Wax Stamp


    Fleur De Lis Design




    My final design in the Fusion 360 program

    Using 2d shapes, extrusions, filets, and other tools within Fusion 360, I designed this ornate stamp. After getting the hang of the program, this took probably 20 minutes to create. To achieve the "P" and the Fleur De Lis on the bottom (as seen in the last picture below), I imported photos, traced around them with line segments, then extruded those 2D shapes into what they are now.
