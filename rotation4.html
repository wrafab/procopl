3D Printing


My first task during this rotation was to create a 3D printed bust of my
own head. I started this process by first getting a scan done of my head
and getting that file into meshmixer.  Once in meshmixer, I was able to
shape the head up and combine it with a pillar that I found online.  This
was my completed bust.  It took a few hours to print and then I had my bust!
