My first task during this rotation was to make a simple, cardboard box that
was fit together using a design and laser cut.  I rastered a skull and crossbones
on the front, fitting with my pirate theme.
			


My second task was to make a press-fit kit, a kind of cheap lego.  I simply made the
shapes I wanted in coreldraw and then drew .25 inch slots on each side of the shapes.
It came out well and worked.  Here's the kit:
			


My third task was to create a coaster made from cork.  I created a sailboat design in
coreldraw and rastered that onto the cork circle.  It came out clean and has functioned
as a coaster in my room since. Here it is:
			


My final laser cutting task was to create my own rubber bracelet.  I decided to go with a
pirate theme with this as well. I used the wooden guide block and the bracelet to measure and
then I made a design on coreldraw. It took me two attempts to perfect this but it came out well
in the end. Here it is:
